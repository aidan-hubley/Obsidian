# Qualities Associated with Terrorists
### Class Suggestions
- Death
- Terror
- Motive
- Lack remorse
- Leverage fear
- Intelligent (deviance)
- Mentally Ill
- Willing to use force
- Seeking power / revenge
- Genetic component
- Either are or have experience ***Powerless***
